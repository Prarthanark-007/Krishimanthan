“We developed an application to help farmers get better prices for their crops.
Farmers register using their RTC number. Based on soil fertility and water resources, the app suggests suitable crops.
It also provides information about crop diseases and their treatment methods.
After harvesting, farmers can check market prices through the app.
If the market price is low, they can sell their produce directly to the government.
The government can then distribute it for export, import, or for mid-day meal schemes in schools.
If the farmer gets a good market price, they can sell it in the market, but the transaction must be updated in the application.”
